1.3 Headings and Paragraphs

Heading

What looked like a small patch of purple grass, above five feet square,
was moving across the sand in their direction.

When it came near enough he perceived that it was not grass;
there were no blades, but only purple roots. The roots were revolving,
for each small plant in the whole patch, like the spokes of a rimless wheel.
